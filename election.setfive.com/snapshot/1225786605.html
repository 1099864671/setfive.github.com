Election Day - Who is winning what states? What does Twitter have to say?

At about midnight today we decided it may be interesting to see what states are voting what according to monitoring twitter. We've written a few algorithms that parse many of the posts to twitter for certain content, to determine if people are stating they are supporting a candidate or if they voted for a specific one. As the states get darker red or blue, you can see who Twitter is saying is going to win the state. So far we have recorded 32 inputs from twitter.

Since we did write this in about 2 hours, we can't promise that it is accurate, but thought it'd be interesting.
