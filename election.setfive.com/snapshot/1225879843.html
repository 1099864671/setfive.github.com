Election Day - Who is winning which states? What does Twitter have to say?

At about midnight today, we decided to see what states are voting what according to monitoring twitter. We've written a few algorithms that parse many tweets for certain content,looking to estimate who twitter users support in the election. As the relative volume of tweets supporting a candidate grows, the state in question gets darker red or darker blue. So far we have recorded 10600 inputs from twitter. Results will update automatically every 10 seconds.

Currently we have 2410 tweets for the republicans and 8190 for the democrats.

Since we did write this in about 2 hours, we can't promise that it is accurate, but thought it'd be interesting. Leave us comments over on our blog post.
